Polar bears live on sea ice across the Arctic.
They use the ice as a platform for hunting seals.
As spring arrives earlier in some regions, the hunting season becomes shorter.
Scientists have observed that some bears now travel farther to find food.
These changes may affect cub survival in the long term.
