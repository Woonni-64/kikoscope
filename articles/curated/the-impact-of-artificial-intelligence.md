Artificial intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines, especially computer systems.

These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

AI has become a critical part of the technology industry.

It is used in many applications such as natural language processing, speech recognition, and machine vision.

The development of AI has led to significant advancements in various fields including healthcare, finance, and transportation.

Machine learning, a subset of AI, allows systems to learn from data without being explicitly programmed.

Deep learning, a further subset, uses neural networks with multiple layers to analyze data.

These technologies are changing the way we live and work, offering new opportunities and challenges.
