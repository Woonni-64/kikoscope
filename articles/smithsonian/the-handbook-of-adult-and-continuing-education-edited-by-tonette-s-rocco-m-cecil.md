With fewer U.S. high school graduates on the horizon, higher education institutions will need to attract \"non-traditional\" (i.e., older) adult learners to remain viable, both financially and politically. There is a need to develop a better corpus of scholarship on topics as diverse as, what learning theories are useful for understanding adult learning?
