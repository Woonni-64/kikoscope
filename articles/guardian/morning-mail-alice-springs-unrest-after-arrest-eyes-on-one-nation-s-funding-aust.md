<p>Morning everyone. Police have used teargas to control rioters in Alice Springs who laid siege to the town’s hospital, where a man arrested last night in connection with the death of five-year-old Kumanjayi Little Baby was being held.</p> 
<p>We also have a special report on Gina Rinehart’s growing financial backing of Pauline Hanson’s One Nation, former oil and gas chiefs warning the government must not prioritise new fossil fuel projects, and why it’s good to invest in Australian comedy.</p> 
<h2>Australia</h2> 
<ul> 
 <li><p><em><strong>Alice Springs arrest | </strong></em>Northern Territory police <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/2026/apr/30/jefferson-lewis-arrested-over-death-of-kumanjayi-little-baby-in-northern-territory">have arrested </a>Jefferson Lewis, 47, in connection with the death of five-year-old Kumanjayi Little Baby, sparking turbulent scenes in Alice Springs outside the hospital where he was taken for treatment. It is understood police acted after a tipoff from community members and he was found at a house in Alice Springs at about 10pm last night.</p></li> 
 <li><p><em><strong>Exclusive | </strong></em>Former oil and gas industry leaders, including senior executives from BP and Shell, <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/environment/2026/may/01/australians-will-pay-more-if-albanese-fast-tracks-fossil-fuel-projects-former-oil-and-gas-leaders-warn">are warning</a> the Albanese government that Australians risk ongoing price shocks and higher costs if it prioritises fossil fuel development in response to the global energy crisis. Deloitte has meanwhile said that limiting changes to the CGT discount and negative gearing rules to new investments would <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/2026/apr/30/limiting-capital-gains-tax-changes-to-new-investments-would-severely-delay-budget-reforms-deloitte-says">“severely delay”</a> desperately needed budget reforms.</p></li> 
 <li><p><em><strong>Palantir plea | </strong></em>The US spy tech company Palantir says it is just <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2026/apr/30/palantir-manifesto-australia-government-contracts">“a software company”</a> amid calls for Australian government agencies to ban new contracts with the controversial company. This comes weeks after Palantir caused a storm in the UK by issuing a manifesto that implied some cultures are inferior to others.</p></li> 
 <li><p><em><strong>Bondi pushback | </strong></em>Queensland <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/australia-news/2026/apr/30/queensland-rejects-key-bondi-report-recommendation-as-albaneses-gun-buyback-flounders">has rejected</a> key recommendations from the Bondi royal commission’s interim report, insisting plans for a national gun buyback will not keep weapons “out of the hands of terrorists and criminals”.</p></li> 
 <li><p><em><strong>Eating in | </strong></em>Shares in companies making pizza, fried chicken and doughnuts have <a href="https://www.theguardian.com/business/2026/apr/30/asx-fast-food-shares-down-cost-of-living-australia-dominos-kfc">fallen sharply</a> on the ASX as households trim their budgets amid the growing cost-of-living crisis.</p></li> 
</ul>
